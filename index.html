Why use the Community
Recycling Centres?

Most of the waste accepted at Community Recycling Centres can be reused or recycled. By sorting your waste and taking it to your local centre, you will:

  • Help improve resource recovery rates
  • Save water, energy and other valuable natural resources


Storing and transporting
your wastes

Handle and transport your materials carefully and safely. Protect your vehicle by placing items on a protective sheet or try in the boot to capture any leakages or breakages.



For more information call the Environment Line on 131 555, contact your local
council or visit www.epa.nsw.gov.au/recyclingcentres
